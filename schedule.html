Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How long does the scheduling take?

Just a few minutes! Fill out the form with your preferred dates and times, and we'll confirm your appointment.

❓ Can I change my scheduled time?

Yes! Contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled pickup or delivery to make changes.

❓ What if I'm not home during pickup?

Please note access instructions in the special instructions field. You can also arrange a different time in the form.

❓ Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! We offer express service for urgent needs. Select express service in the order form or contact us directly.

❓ What areas do you service?

We provide pickup and delivery services throughout Abuja. Contact us to confirm your location is within our service area.
